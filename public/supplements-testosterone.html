Important Safety Warning

Men under 25: Your testosterone levels are naturally at their peak. These supplements are generally unnecessary and could interfere with natural hormone production. Consult a healthcare provider before use.

Always get lab testing: Test your testosterone levels before and during supplementation. Work with a healthcare professional for proper monitoring.

Medical Disclaimer & Lab Testing

Testosterone supplementation requires medical supervision. Get baseline hormone panels including Total T, Free T, LH, FSH, and Estradiol. These supplements may interact with medications and are not appropriate for everyone. Never exceed recommended dosages.

Final Safety Reminders

These supplements are not magic bullets. Proper sleep (7-9 hours), regular strength training, stress management, and optimal nutrition are far more important for healthy testosterone levels.

Start with one supplement at a time to assess individual response. Many of these can be combined, but begin conservatively.

If you're under 25 with normal energy and libido, focus on lifestyle factors rather than supplementation.
