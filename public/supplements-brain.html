Important Considerations

Synergistic Effects: Many supplements work better in combination. Consider omega-3 + curcumin, or alpha-GPC + phosphatidylserine.
Quality Matters: Choose reputable brands with third-party testing. Avoid proprietary blends with undisclosed amounts.
Individual Variation: Effects can vary significantly between individuals. Start with lower doses and monitor response.
Lifestyle Factors: Supplements work best alongside good sleep, exercise, stress management, and a healthy diet.
